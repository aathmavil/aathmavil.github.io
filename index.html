About

Aathmavil Manjupeyyumbol (ആത്മാവിൽ മഞ്ഞുപെയ്യുമ്പോൾ, translation: snowing in the soul) is a Malayalam podcast started by four college friends from Kerala, India. Bestin came up with the idea of creating a podcast when he first heard about the hosting platform Anchor. The name was chosen by Fr. Saiju. The podcast aims to refresh souls with the snow of happiness and peace. In each episode, we will cover a story—fiction or real-life—and explains how it can inspire our lives. Our podcast is available on 50+ platforms. So, you can listen to the latest episodes anywhere, anytime on the go.
